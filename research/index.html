Statistical Optimal Transport

Suppose that \(X, X_1, X_2, \ldots\) are i.i.d. random elements on a probability space \((\Omega, \mathscr F, \mathbb P)\) taking values in a measurable space \((\mathsf S, \mathscr S)\) with distribution \(\mu := \mathbb P \circ X^{-1}.\)
